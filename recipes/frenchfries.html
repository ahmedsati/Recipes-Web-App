French Fries
Description

These Homemade French Fries are SO much better than what you can get at a restaurant! The secret is to make double fried french fries cooked at two temperatures, which gives you perfect crispy edges and a fluffy interior.

There's nothing more deflating than going to all the effort of making your own fries from scratch, only to find they start losing crispiness before they even hit the table. But this method, following the steps below is the perfect approach to make tasty fries!

Ingredients
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons Vinegar
  • A tablespoon of Salt
  • Oil in a pan to fry
Steps

Here are the instuctions to follow in order to make the fries I bet you're craving!

  1. Cut Fries into equal lengths
  2. Keep in water to prevent browning
  3. Rinse well to remove dirt
  4. Cook in Vinegar Water == Crispiness!
  5. Drain water and let it dry
  6. Cooking vessel and oil
  7. First round of frying
  8. Cool for 30 minutes
  9. Second Fry == Extra Crispy!
  10. Season!
